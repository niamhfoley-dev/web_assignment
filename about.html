About EqualTickets

Our Mission

At EqualTickets, we believe in fairness, transparency, and accessibility in ticketing. Our mission is to create a marketplace where fans can buy and sell resale tickets for comedy, music, and sports events at prices that reflect the true value of the experience, not the whims of scalpers and bots.

Who We Are

EqualTickets was founded by a team of avid event-goers who were frustrated with the current state of the ticketing industry. We understand the joy of attending live events and the disappointment that comes when tickets are priced out of reach. That’s why we created EqualTickets – to ensure that everyone has a fair chance to enjoy their favorite performances and games.

What We Do

EqualTickets is a platform dedicated to offering a fair and transparent marketplace for ticket resales. We provide:

  • Fair Pricing: Our algorithms and policies ensure that ticket prices are fair and reflect true market demand, protecting buyers from excessive markups.
  • Verified Tickets: All tickets sold on EqualTickets are verified to ensure authenticity, giving you peace of mind that your purchase is legitimate.
  • User-Friendly Experience: Our website and app are designed to be intuitive and easy to use, making it simple to find and purchase tickets for the events you love.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated support team is here to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Why Choose Us

  • Transparency: We are committed to providing clear and upfront information about ticket pricing and availability.
  • Security: Our platform uses the latest security measures to protect your personal and payment information.
  • Community: We believe in fostering a community of fans who share our passion for live events. Our platform includes features that allow users to connect, share experiences, and support one another.

Join Us
